Keybinds:
-  1, 2, 3, 4: tabs control
-  SPACE: play/pause
-  [<], [>]: backward/forward
-  DEL while on playlist tab: clear playlist
-  ESC while on album page: go back to album list
-  ESC while focused searchbar: unfocus it
-  SHIFT while scrolling volume: change by 1%
